THALIA

Thalia is a name of Greek origin, referring to the Muse of comedy and bucolic poetry in Greek mythology. It also means "to flourish" or "blooming," symbolizing growth, prosperity, and vitality.

ELEOS

Eleos is a Greek word meaning "mercy" or "compassion," representing an act of kindness and understanding towards those in distress or suffering, reflecting a tender and caring nature.

AGAPI

Agapi is a Greek word that translates to "unconditional love" or "selfless love," embodying a deep and profound affection that goes beyond personal gain.

SOPHRONIA

Sophronia is a feminine given name of Greek origin, derived from the word "sophrosyne," which conveys the meaning of "wisdom," "moderation," or "prudence,".

THEON

Theon is a name of Greek origin, meaning "godly" or "divine," representing a person with qualities or attributes that are godlike or exhibiting exceptional excellence and virtue.

IRENE

Irene is a name of Greek origin, derived from the word "eirēnē," which means "peace," symbolizing a peaceful and serene nature, as well as a desire to promote harmony and tranquility.
